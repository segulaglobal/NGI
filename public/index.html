Every Girl Deserves Her Dreams

Every girl in Northern Ghana deserves the chance to dare to dream. A chance to envision a future full of promise - whether that means becoming a nurse, teacher, innovator, or leader. At Northern Girl Initiative (NGI), we empower these girls to turn their dreams into realities with access to mentors, education programs, and career development opportunities.

We link girls to people and resources that build their confidence and expand their vision of what's possible. When you support a young woman's highest ambitions, you uplift entire communities in the process.
